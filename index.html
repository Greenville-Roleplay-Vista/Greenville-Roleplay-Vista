About Us

Greenville Roleplay Vista is a community-driven roleplay server on Discord. We provide a fun and engaging environment for roleplaying enthusiasts. Our server offers a variety of roleplay scenarios and events, ensuring there's something for everyone.

Server Rules

  • Age Requirement: You must be 13 years of age or older to join the server.
  • Respect Everyone.
  • Keep Discussions Civil.
  • Respect Privacy.
  • No NSFW Content.
  • No Spamming or Self-Promotion.
  • No Drama or Toxicity.
  • Listen to the Staff Team.
  • Follow Discord's Terms of Service.

In-Game Rules

  • Fail Roleplay (FRP): FRP (Fail Roleplay) is not permitted within this community. We strive to maintain immersive and authentic roleplaying experiences for all members, and engaging in FRP detracts from that goal. Please adhere to the established roleplaying standards and guidelines to ensure a positive and enjoyable environment for everyone.
  • Combat Logging: Participants are expected to remain connected during active roleplay scenarios, particularly those involving law enforcement or emergency situations. Disconnecting from the game to evade consequences or interactions, known as Combat Logging, is strictly forbidden.
  • Cop/Staff Baiting: Refrain from intentionally provoking law enforcement officers or server staff members with the aim of eliciting a negative response or encouraging rule breaches. Show respect for the roles and authority of in-game law enforcement and server staff.
  • Vehicle Spawning Rules: Vehicle spawning is only permitted at designated locations, such as the dealership, apartments, claimed houses, friend's houses, and specific commercial areas. Please obtain staff permission via "!Staff Respawn Permissions" for spawning outside these areas, and refrain from spawning vehicles on the streets.
  • Exchanging: When involved in a collision with another driver, it is mandatory to promptly move your vehicle to the side of the road, step out of your car, and exchange information with the other driver by saying "-exchanges-". Failure to comply with this requirement will result in removal from the session and an infraction.
  • Restricted Vehicles: You are only permitted to drive restricted vehicles if you own the appropriate exemptions. Failure to adhere to this rule may result in disciplinary action.
  • Vehicle Slots:
    • What are vehicle slots? Vehicle slots are specific permissions that allow players to use restricted vehicles during a session. Any vehicle listed under "Slotted Vehicles" on the restricted vehicle list is eligible to receive a slot.
    • How do I receive a slot? You can request a vehicle slot for any slotted vehicle by sending a direct message to the host. In your message, specify which slotted vehicle you are interested in driving. If the host grants you a slot, you will be permitted to drive the vehicle you requested for the duration of the session.
  • Public Services: Participation in public services is limited to individuals with the proper authorization. Ensure you possess the required roles before operating as one to maintain the integrity and effectiveness of our community.
  • GvOs Phone: The Events feature on the phone is restricted for general use. Only the host has the authority to grant permissions for its usage during the session. Therefore, please refrain from utilizing the Events feature unless specifically granted permission by the host.
  • Avatar Regulations: To ensure the highest quality experience in our sessions, it is essential to use realistic avatars. This helps create a more immersive and engaging environment.
  • Peacetime Speeds:
    • Strict Peacetime: 65 MPH
    • Normal Peacetime: 80 MPH
    • Peacetime Off: 90 MPH
    • Peacetime Off + Priority: 110 MPH
